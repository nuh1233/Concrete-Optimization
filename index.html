The Need for Construction in Smart Cities

Smart cities often emphasize sensors, AI, and advanced technologies but forget about the **construction** that supports these innovations. **Concrete** remains the backbone of most infrastructure projects. Without proper construction planning, smart city ambitions will remain incomplete. The foundation must be built before smart technologies can truly integrate into urban life.

The Cost of Futuristic Cities: The Line City Example

The **Line City** proposed by Saudi Arabia is a shining example of futuristic urban design. However, the cost of building such cities can be astronomical, running into **hundreds of billions of dollars**. Construction resources like **concrete** and metal need to be optimized at scale to ensure these projects remain financially sustainable. Mismanagement could lead to skyrocketing costs that could derail the vision of the city.
